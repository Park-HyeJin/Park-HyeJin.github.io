APCTP Seminar Series: Modeling on Ecology and Evolution (MEE)

Aim

Individual interactions shape the population-level quantities. We aim to study the macroscopic ecological patterns observed in nature and understand the underlying mechanisms of them. To do that, in this seminar series, we bring physicists and biologists together.
